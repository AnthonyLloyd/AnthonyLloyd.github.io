Find it on NuGet and github.

Key improvements:

  • Improved generation for double, float and decimal. Mix of integer, fraction, exponential and evenly distributed. Much better at finding tricky rounding issues.
  • Improved Print for double, float and decimal. Displays small, rounded fraction and exponential representation.
  • Improved min size passing and shrinking performance.
  • Large performance improvements for Sample and Faster.
  • Plus, many other improvements to make the API easier to use.
It was already the fastest, best at shrinking, and had the most features. Now it's even better.
